Shipping Policy

At Successful Faith, we believe in quality over speed. Unlike fast fashion brands that ship from dusty warehouses, every piece of apparel you order is printed specifically for you.

1. Production Time

Because our items are made-to-order, please allow time for your gear to be created before it ships.

  • Standard Apparel (Tees & Hoodies): 2-5 business days for printing and quality control.
  • During Holidays: Production times may increase slightly due to high volume.

2. Shipping Times

Once your item passes our quality check and leaves the facility, the estimated delivery times are:

  • USA (Standard): 3-7 business days.
  • International: 10-30 business days, depending on customs processing.

Note: You will receive a tracking number via email as soon as your order ships.

3. Lost or Stolen Packages

Successful Faith is not responsible for packages that are marked as "Delivered" by the carrier but are missing. Please ensure your shipping address is secure.

If your package is lost in transit (never marked delivered), please contact us at support@successfulfaith.com so we can assist you with filing a claim.

4. International Duties & Taxes

For orders outside the USA, you may be responsible for import duties and taxes charged by your local customs office. These fees are not included in our shipping price.
