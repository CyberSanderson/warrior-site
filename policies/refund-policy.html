Refund & Shipping Policy

Last updated: December 25, 2025

1. Return Policy (Physical Goods)

We want you to love your Successful Faith apparel. If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, you may return it within 30 days of the delivery date.

To be eligible for a return, your item must be:

  • Unworn and unwashed
  • In the same condition that you received it
  • Free of scents, stains, or hair

Note: Customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the item arrived damaged or defective.

2. Non-Refundable Items (Digital Products)

Due to the immediate nature of digital downloads, all sales of digital products (including PDF Prayer Guides) are final.

Once the file has been downloaded or emailed to you, we cannot offer a refund. However, if you have trouble accessing your file, please contact us immediately and we will ensure you get your copy.

3. How to Start a Return

To initiate a return for physical apparel:

  1. Email us at successfulffaith@gmail.com with your Order Number.
  2. We will provide you with the return shipping address.
  3. Pack the item securely and ship it back to us using a trackable shipping method.

4. Refunds & Processing

Once your return is received and inspected, we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund. If approved, your refund will be processed, and a credit will automatically be applied to your original method of payment within 5-10 business days.

5. Damaged or Defective Items

If you received a misprinted, damaged, or defective item, we will replace it at no cost to you. Please email us a photo of the damage within 7 days of receiving the item.

6. Shipping Information

  • Standard Shipping: Please allow 1-3 business days for processing and 3-7 business days for delivery.
  • Lost Packages: Successful Faith is not responsible for packages that are confirmed delivered by the carrier but are subsequently lost or stolen.
